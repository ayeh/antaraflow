**Deliverables:** DOCX brand book (20 pages, bilingual EN/BM) + markdown reference

## Overview

Complete brand identity for **antaraNote** — a rebrand of antaraFlow, the multi-tenant SaaS platform for formal Minutes of Meeting (MoM) management. Target markets: GLC (Government-Linked Companies), Government, and SME in Malaysia/SEA.

## Brand Name

**antaraNote** (singular, no space)
- "antara" = Malay for "between/among"
- "Note" = meeting notes, minutes, documentation
- Lowercase "antara" + title case "Note" — approachable yet anchored

## 1. Brand Strategy

### Brand Story
"antara" (between/among) is the strategic anchor. antaraNote positions itself as the bridge *between* what was discussed and what gets done. Between people, between decisions, between meetings.

### Tagline
**Primary:** "Between Words and Action" / *"Antara Kata dan Tindakan"*
**Secondary:** "Where Decisions Are Documented" / *"Di Mana Keputusan Didokumentasikan"*

### Brand Archetype
**The Sage** (with Ruler undertones)
- Trustworthy, authoritative, structured
- The Sage seeks truth and documentation
- Ruler undertone adds governance credibility for GLC/Gov buyers
- NOT a playful startup brand — a platform boards and ministries trust

### Voice & Tone Matrix

| Dimension | Always | Never |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Tone | Professional, assured, clear | Casual, slang, hype |
| Language | Precise, structured, bilingual | Vague, overly technical |
| Personality | Dependable advisor | Trendy disruptor |
| Authority | Governance-literate, compliant | Preachy, bureaucratic |

### Messaging Hierarchy
1. **Primary:** Formal meeting documentation, automated and trusted
2. **Secondary:** AI-powered efficiency — transcription, extraction, action tracking
3. **Tertiary:** Governance compliance, audit trails, enterprise security

## 2. Logo — Direction B: "The Quorum Dot"

### Concept
A circular mark built from 3 overlapping circles in a Venn-diagram arrangement — representing people coming together (quorum). The center intersection is the "note" — the shared truth that emerges from the meeting.

**Wordmark:** `antara` in lowercase, `Note` in title case. No space: `antaraNote`.
**Personality:** Collaborative, human, governance-aware.

### Logo Variations
- **Primary lockup:** Icon + wordmark (horizontal)
- **Stacked lockup:** Icon above wordmark (vertical)
- **Icon only:** For favicon, app icon, small spaces
- **Wordmark only:** For inline text, partnerships
- **Reverse:** White on dark backgrounds
- **Monochrome:** Single-color for print constraints

### Usage Rules
- Minimum clear space = height of the "a" in antaraNote on all sides
- Minimum size = 24px height (digital), 10mm (print)
- Never rotate, stretch, recolor with non-brand colors, or add effects
- Always use on solid backgrounds — no busy photography behind logo

## 5. Imagery & Illustration Style

### Photography
- Clean, high-key corporate photography with teal/navy color grading
- Boardrooms, meeting tables, collaboration, screens with dashboards
- Slight desaturation with teal duotone overlay
- Diverse Malaysian/SEA representation — hijab, various ethnicities, government/modern office settings

### Icons & Illustrations
- Line icons: 1.5px stroke, rounded corners, teal primary
- Spot illustrations: Geometric, flat, brand palette — empty states, onboarding, errors
- No gradients, no 3D, no isometric

### Patterns
- **Quorum Pattern:** Repeating overlapping circles (from logo) at 5% opacity — headers, login screens, presentations
- **Grid dots:** Light dot grid for document/note sections
